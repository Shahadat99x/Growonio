# Growonio — Master Context

## 1. Project Summary

Growonio is a modern digital solutions studio focused on helping service businesses grow through practical digital systems.

Primary market: Romania  
Primary languages: Romanian and English  
Primary positioning: business-focused websites, booking systems, lead capture, client management, and simple automation

Main brand line:
**Growonio — Business automation built for growth**

This project is not a generic portfolio site. It is a conversion-focused company website for a real business that will sell digital services to local and regional clients.

---

## 2. Core Positioning

Growonio should be positioned as a focused, modern, trustworthy digital studio for service businesses.

We do not want to sound like:

- a cheap freelancer
- a fake large agency
- a vague AI company
- a generic software house that builds everything

We want to sound like:

- practical
- modern
- premium but approachable
- business-focused
- outcome-driven
- Romania-ready
- bilingual
- SEO-aware

Main value proposition:
We help service businesses get more leads, improve booking flows, reduce manual work, and present themselves professionally online.

---

## 3. Ideal Client Profile

Growonio is best suited for service-based businesses, especially those with appointments, inquiries, or repeat customers.

Priority target segments:

- salons and beauty businesses
- clinics and appointment-based health services
- consultants and service firms
- local businesses that need lead capture and workflow improvement

These clients often need:

- a modern website
- service presentation
- pricing display
- lead capture forms
- WhatsApp/contact integration
- booking and scheduling
- customer management basics
- reminders or simple automation
- bilingual presentation
- local SEO readiness

---

---

## 5. Website Goals

The Growonio website must do 5 things well:

1. Make the company look real, modern, and trustworthy
2. Explain clearly what problems Growonio solves
3. Support organic SEO from the beginning
4. Help qualify leads through services and pricing
5. Make it easy for visitors to contact or request a quote

This is not a site built only for visual beauty. It must support sales and long-term discoverability.

---

## 6. Language Strategy

Growonio should be bilingual.

Required languages:

- Romanian (primary / default)
- English (secondary)

Romanian matters for:

- local trust
- local SEO
- easier sales to Romanian businesses

English matters for:

- broader credibility
- future international positioning
- portfolio sharing
- founders / expats / non-Romanian business owners

Preferred routing:

- `/` or `/ro` = Romanian
- `/en` = English

Important rules:

- translations must be proper, not sloppy
- metadata must be localized
- CTA labels must be localized
- key pages must exist in both languages

## 10. SEO Strategy Summary

SEO must be built in from the beginning.

Main SEO principles:

- Romanian-first local organic strategy
- English as secondary international support
- semantic page structure
- fast pages
- clean URLs
- strong metadata
- internal linking
- FAQ content
- industry relevance
- service relevance
- local-business discoverability

Priority SEO targets:

- web design / website creation for service businesses
- booking systems
- bilingual websites
- lead capture / automation
- solution pages for niches like salons / clinics / consultants

Technical SEO requirements:

- dynamic metadata
- sitemap
- robots.txt
- canonical tags
- hreflang
- Open Graph tags
- structured data
- image alt text
- clean headings

## 11. Admin / CMS Scope

Use a hybrid approach:

- layouts and system logic stay coded
- frequently changing content is editable in admin

Editable from admin:

- services content
- pricing packages
- portfolio items
- blog / insights
- testimonials
- FAQs
- company info
- selected homepage content

Hardcoded / controlled in code:

- layout system
- nav/footer structure
- design system
- route structure
- SEO logic
- hreflang logic
- schema logic

## 12. Suggested Tech Stack

Preferred stack:

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase
- Cloudinary
- Vercel

Likely additions:

- next-intl or similar for i18n
- Resend only if needed for transactional form emails
- domain email preferred for business communication
- React Hook Form + Zod for forms
- shadcn/ui if useful for clean component base
- analytics (Google Analytics and Search Console)
- simple CMS/admin using Supabase-backed protected dashboard

Notes:

- domain-based business email is preferred over generic free mail
- avoid overengineering
- focus on speed, maintainability, and clean UI

---

## 13. Project Constraints

Important rules:

- do not turn the site into a giant generic agency site
- do not make every UI section CMS-editable
- do not make AI-themed vague marketing the core identity
- do not overpromise services
- do not sacrifice clarity for fancy design
- do not ignore Romanian local SEO
- do not rely on blog content alone for traffic

---

## 14. Non-Goals

At current stage, Growonio is NOT trying to be:

- a giant enterprise software company
- a SaaS product platform
- a no-code marketplace
- a full multi-role agency with dozens of services
- a fake big corporation

Growonio should launch as a focused, credible, boutique digital studio.

---

## 15. Success Criteria

A successful Growonio website should:

- look premium and modern
- clearly explain services
- support Romanian and English
- rank over time for relevant service and local intent
- allow easy content management for key areas
- help close real business leads
- be easy to maintain and extend
